WRAITH

Wraith is an Offensive Legend. She is a versatile Legend, Wraith can reposition and flank enemies with her abilities. Wraith's tactical ability called “Into the Void” allows her to reposition or take cover while being invincible for 4 seconds. Wraith's ultimate ability called “Dimensional Rift” places an entrance and exit portal that allows her squad to be safely transported over long distances. Her passive “Voices from the Void” gives her and her teammates audible warnings about enemies aiming at them.
  • Passive Ability

    Voices from the Void

  • Tactical Ability

    Into the Void

  • Ultimate Ability

    Dimensional Rift

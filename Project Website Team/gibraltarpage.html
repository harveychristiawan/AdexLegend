GIBRALTAR

Gibraltar is a Defensive Legend. He is a walking fortress Legend with defensive and protection ability. His tactical ability “Dome of Protection” creates a temporary half-dome shield that can be walked through, but not fired through. His passive “Gun Shield” causes a shield to form around the upper half of his body whenever he aims down sights; this shield takes some damage before breaking. His ultimate ability “Defensive Bombardment” causes a damaging and stunning cluster strike to land on a targeted location.
  • Passive Ability

    Gun Shield

  • Tactical Ability

    Dome of Shield

  • Ultimate Ability

    Defensive Bombardment
